Requirements

To become a Specialist Paramedic, there are a few essential requirements and qualifications needed:

  • A valid paramedic qualification (Health & Care Professions Council registered)
  • Minimum GCSEs of grade 9-4 (A*-C) including Maths, English, and Science
  • Experience as a paramedic with at least 1-2 years of practice
  • Completion of additional specialized training (Critical Care Paramedic, Advanced Life Support, etc.)
  • Ability to work under pressure and in high-stress environments
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills
